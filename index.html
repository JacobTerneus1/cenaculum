About

What Cenaculum is

A retreat built around prayer, the liturgy, and life together in Latin.

Prayer and liturgy

Mass, the divine office, and common prayer shape the day. Cenaculum is meant to make room for worship without turning the retreat into a rush.

Latin all day

Latin is the ordinary language of Cenaculum. We ask participants to use it throughout the retreat except in emergencies or matters of real necessity.

Shared rhythm

Meals, talks, games, walks, and quiet time are all part of the retreat. The structure is steady, but the atmosphere is relaxed.

De Cenaculo

Quid Cenaculum sit

Recessus circa precationem, liturgiam, atque vitam communem Latine ducendam constitutus.

Precatio et liturgia

Missa, officium divinum, atque oratio communis diem informant. Cenaculum ita ordinatur ut cultui divino locus largus detur, sine tamen festinatione.

Latine per diem

Latina lingua ordinaria Cenaculi est. Petimus ut ea per totum Cenaculum utamini, nisi in extremis vel in rebus valde necessariis.

Ritus communes

Ientaculum, prandium, cena, sermones, ludi, deambulationes, atque tempora quietis omnia ad recessum pertinent. Ordo stabilis est; animus tamen remissus manet.

Practical notes

What to expect and what to bring

Clothing

In the chapel, please wear clothing appropriate to the chapel. Otherwise, wear whatever suits the place and the activity. We plan to walk outside from time to time on easy trails, but most of Cenaculum will take place indoors.

Food

Meals will be provided three times each day: breakfast, lunch, and dinner. If you prefer to eat more than three times a day, bring extra food for your own needs. Coffee will certainly be available in the morning, and perhaps throughout the day.

If you need special food for health reasons and have not yet indicated this in your registration, please tell Abbot Jeffrey right away: retreatcenter@icksp.org.

Things to bring

  • A rosary
  • A notebook or pages for notes
  • A dictionary or other Latin books
  • A musical instrument, if it will help you sing at the final evening celebration
  • Books from the Family of Saint Jerome

Speaking expectations

Please take care to speak only Latin throughout Cenaculum, except in emergencies or in matters of real necessity.

Monita practica

Quid exspectandum sit et quid apportandum

Vestimenta

In sacello, quaeso, gerite vestimenta sacello digna. Alioquin, gerite quod loco et actioni conveniat. In animo habemus interdum foris ambulare in callibus lenibus, sed maior pars Cenaculi intus habebitur.

Cibus

Ter in singulis diebus fercula nobis afferentur, videlicet: ientaculum, prandium, atque cena. Si mavultis plus quam ter in die comedere, quaeso afferte cibum superadditum pro necessitatibus vestris. Coffea, multis, me incluso, potio necessaria est; ergo certo mane aderit, et fortasse per totum diem.

Si cibo peculiari vobis opus est sanitatis causa, et hoc nondum in inscriptione vestra indicavistis, quaeso dicite Abbati Jeffrey confestim: retreatcenter@icksp.org.

Res apportandae

  • Rosarium
  • Paginas vel librum in quo notas scribere potestis
  • Lexicon vel alios libros Latinos
  • Instrumentum musicale, si vos adiuvabit ad cantandum in celebratione ultimorum vesperorum
  • Libri Familiae Sancti Hieronimi

Lingua loquenda

Bene curate, quaeso, ut per totum Cenaculum tantum Latine loquamini, nisi in extremis vel in rebus valde necessariis.

FAQ

Common questions

What language should I speak?

Please speak Latin throughout Cenaculum except in emergencies or matters of real necessity.

What should I bring?

A rosary, a notebook, a dictionary or other Latin books, and any musical instrument that may help you sing at the final evening celebration.

What if I need special food or travel help?

For special dietary needs, tell Abbot Jeffrey right away at retreatcenter@icksp.org. For travel plans, contact Jackson Perry as soon as possible.

Quaestiones communes

Qua lingua loqui debeo?

Quaeso per totum Cenaculum tantum Latine loquimini, nisi in extremis vel in rebus valde necessariis.

Quid mecum apportare debeo?

Rosarium, paginas vel librum ad notas scribendas, lexicon vel alios libros Latinos, atque instrumentum musicale, si ad ultimam vespertinam celebrationem adiuvabit.

Quid si cibo peculiari aut auxilio itineris egeo?

Si cibo peculiari vobis opus est sanitatis causa, statim dicite Abbati Jeffrey ad retreatcenter@icksp.org. De consiliis itineris, Jacobulum Perry quam primum certiorem facite.
